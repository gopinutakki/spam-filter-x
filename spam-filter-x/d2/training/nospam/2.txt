Dear Google Account holder:

2-step verification was turned off for your account. We are sorry to
see you go. If you did not turn off 2-step, or want to turn 2-step
verification back on, visit:
https://www.google.com/accounts/SmsAuthConfig

If you have turned off 2-step verification because your phone or
applications could no longer access your Google Account, learn how to
fix these by using application-specific passwords:
http://www.google.com/support/accounts/bin/answer.py?answer=185833

Now that you've turned off 2-step verification, revoke any
application-specific passwords you created to authorize applications
and devices to access your Google Account. To revoke
application-specific passwords, visit:
https://www.google.com/accounts/IssuedAuthSubTokens

If you have any suggestions for how we can improve 2-step
verification, please let us know:
http://www.google.com/support/accounts/bin/request.py?contact_type=2step_feedback


If you have questions about how 2-step verification works or want information about additional
steps to keep your account secure, you can learn more at the Help Center:
http://www.google.com/support/accounts/bin/answer.py?answer=180744&hl=en
